From Their Request to Your Commission

No more wasted time. No more tire-kickers. Just real conversations with real buyers who are ready to enroll today.

1

They Reach Out for Coverage

A real person submits a request specifically asking to speak with a licensed agent about ACA, Medicare, or Final Expense coverage. This isn't a form fill from someone researching options — they want to enroll now.

2

We Screen & Verify First

Before you ever hear their voice, we confirm eligibility, verify they're actively shopping (not just browsing), and ensure they meet your criteria. You skip the tire-kickers, window-shoppers, and "let me think about it" conversations.

3

Live Transfer to Your Phone

The moment they're verified and ready, we connect them directly to you via live phone transfer. No waiting. No follow-up emails. No "we'll call you back." You're talking to a qualified, ready-to-buy prospect right now.

4

You Close & We Invoice

If you enroll them — congratulations, you just made money. We invoice you for that successful call. If you don't close the sale? You pay nothing. Zero. Zilch. That's the beauty of performance-based pricing: you only pay when you win.
